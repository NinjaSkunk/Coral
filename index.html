Mapping our Ocean's Coral Reefs

One Picture at a Time

The Coral Application allows anyone to take pictures of the world's coral reefs. Our machine learning model will then identify if the picture is healthy coral or unhealthy coral. This open sourced project will help track the overall health of our precious coral reefs. Soon, users will be able to see a map of the world's coral reefs based on open sourced pictures.
